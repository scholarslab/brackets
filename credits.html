The Praxis Program

The Praxis Program is a one-year fellowship in which six graduate students from different disciplines spend the fall semester learning digital humanities theory and methods, then the spring semester collaborating to create their own DH project. We are grateful we had the opportunity to participate in this unique program.

Inspiration

  • The Undercommons: Fugitive Planning & Black Study, Stefano Harney and Fred Moten

  • The Three Dimensions of Inclusive Design, Jutta Treviranus
  • Digital Black Feminism, Catherine Knight Steele
